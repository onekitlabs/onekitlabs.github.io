Thank you for using our applications and games, developed by OneKit Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

OneKit Labs is the developer of simple applications and classic games. For questions about this policy, contact us at onekitlabs@gmail.com.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Non-Personal Information:

  • Personal Information (if applicable):
    • Advertising ID (for personalized ads, if enabled).

We use the data we collect to:

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we process data based on:

  • Consent: For personalized ads or data collection requiring explicit permission.
  • Legitimate Interests: For non-personal data used to improve the Game or ensure its functionality.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws.

5. Underage Users

If you are under 16 in the EEA/UK (or under 13 in the US), we require verifiable parental consent to process your personal data, as required by GDPR and COPPA. We tag users aged 13–18 for special handling to limit data collection or ad personalization unless consent is provided. If you are a parent or guardian, please contact us at onekitlabs@gmail.com to provide or revoke consent.

We may use third-party services that collect data, such as:

These services have their own privacy policies, which you can review at:

  • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA/UK, you have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to data processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., for personalized ads).

To exercise these rights, contact us at onekitlabs@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Non-personal data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.
