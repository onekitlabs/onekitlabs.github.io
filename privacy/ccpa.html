If you are a parent or guardian, please contact us at onekitlabs@gmail.com to provide or revoke consent.

6. Your CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request details about the categories of personal information we collect, use, or share.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: Opt out of the sale of your personal information (e.g., disable personalized ads).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights or opt out of personalized ads (“Do Not Sell My Personal Information”), contact us at onekitlabs@gmail.com or adjust your ad settings in the App. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.
